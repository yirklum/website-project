Welcome to Collecting the News, a virtual museum dedicated to some of my favorite things: television news, history and food. Enjoy!

Galleries

Explore galleries full of artifacts and memorabilia acquired during a journalistic career spanning four decades. These collections include a wide range of items central to historic news events, from wars to elections to natural disasters.

View the collection

This Just In

This monthly blog highlights the acquisition of new memorabilia and offers notes on experiences and observations related to television news.

And That's The Way It Was

This space is dedicated to the early days of television news. See photos of events, personalities and equipment that played a role in the development of the industry and discover little-known and interesting facts.

For More On The Story

Read interesting stories about how a particular piece of memorabilia was obtained or, on occasion, how an item was sought after but eventually lost.

News Feed

One of the greatest side benefits of chasing news all over the country is the opportunity to discover unique restaurants and regional cooking. This section features some of the hidden (and not so hidden) treasures uncovered during road trips around the country.

Talking Heads

When Ted Turner introduced CNN to the world he insisted that, in his programming, "the news was the star, not the person reading the news." However, over the years CNN has featured an all-star cast of reporters and anchors that have contributed to the success of the company. Read vignettes on some of the more notable personalities.

Newsgathering Tools

Equipment is the heart and soul of a newsgathering operation. From the beginning CNN has attempted to keep its cameras, editing and transmission gear state-of-the-art. The 24-hour news operation has helped push the envelope of TV technology by assisting in the development of equipment. Discover some of the tools CNN has used over the years.
