Although CNN didn’t launch until June 1980, I’ve tried to collect material from earlier Presidential cycles. Hopefully, these items will add prospective to the Post 1980 material. The items were obtained from numerous sources, including other collectors, auction sites, antique shops and online sites. With so many years to choose from, this section should grow significantly in the future.

Row 1: Theodore Roosevelt in 1885; John F. Kennedy being interviewed by Walter Cronkite; Warren G. Harding at the White House; Row 2: Joseph Stalin, Franklin D. Roosevelt and Winston Churchill; William Howard Taft in Manassas; Calvin Coolidge in 1925.
